Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 2-3 business days within the Philippines. For international orders, delivery may take 7-14 business days, depending on your location.

Do you offer express shipping?

Yes, we offer express shipping for an additional fee. Express orders are delivered within 1-2 business days locally.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the courier's platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods: Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, GCash, and Bank Transfers.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use SSL encryption to ensure your payment information is secure. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 3-5-day return policy for unused and unopened items. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Can I exchange an item?

No, we do not offer exchanges as all sales are final. We recommend reviewing product details carefully before purchasing. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

Are your products safe for babies?

Absolutely! All our products are made from non-toxic, hypoallergenic materials and are rigorously tested to ensure they meet safety standards.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours of placing it. After that, the order will be processed and cannot be changed.
